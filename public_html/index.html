Well...we all know San Francisco. Who would not recognize those fancy cable cars or the famous Golden Gate Bridge. However, San Francisco is one of the most beautiful cities in the United States and the jewel of Northern California. The city is full of history, great neighborhoods, parks, beaches, museums, and a whole host of entertainment options. Some of the most notable attractions, beyond the famous bridge, are historic Alcatraz Island and Fisherman's Wharf. But despite all these facts I want to ask you one question - did you ever think of the amount of films that were made in San Francisco?

Not only big producers like Walt Disney or Warner Bros are interested in producing their films in San Francisco. Also companies like Amazon and Netflix or the American Broadcasting Company started to produce their TV series due to the location's beautiful scenery.
